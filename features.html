The birth pipeline

Your clients move through birth stages, not a sales funnel.

Onboarding, Active, On Call, In Labor, Postpartum. Every other CRM makes you translate your work into "lead" and "customer." This one already speaks it.

  • Gestation on every row. Due date and current week, always visible. You never do the math again.
  • One profile per client. Details, appointments, notes, and documents together, not in three notebooks.
  • A calendar that knows the difference between a prenatal visit, a postpartum check-in, and a birth.

Includes messaging: one thread per client, in the app, instead of scattered texts.

Onboarding a client

Send a link. She fills it out. It's already filed.

No PDF to email, no retyping her answers into your system, no chasing. The intake form lands in her profile the moment she hits submit.

  • Self-serve intake. One link, filled out on her phone, filed automatically.
  • Documents in one place. Birth plans, contracts, forms: two clicks, not twenty.
  • E-signature built in. Contracts signed without a second subscription. 50 per doula per year.

The client portal

The part that makes you look established.

Every client gets her own private space: her appointments, her documents, and a week-by-week look at how baby is growing. Free for her, nothing to install, works in any browser.

  • Week-by-week tracking. "About the size of a grapefruit at week 22 of 40."
  • Your logo, not ours. It reads as your practice, because it is.
  • Private to each client. Isolation enforced at the database, on every request.

Most doula tools simply don't have this.

Getting paid

She taps Pay. The money goes straight to you.

Connect your own PayPal and every invoice gets a Pay button, in the PDF and in her portal. DoulaFlow never holds or processes your money, and never takes a percentage of it. Not a reduced one. None.

  • Your account, your rates. We're not in the payment path. Stripe support is coming.
  • Packages and usage. Build your service packages, track what's been used per client.
  • Insights included. Clients, revenue, activity, at a glance, not as a paid add-on.

Prefer to collect the way you already do? DoulaFlow still builds and tracks the invoice.
